Technology and Innovations

Staying true to our origins that are deeply rooted in technology, Ruder Finn's largest practice in India is Technology First spearheaded by Shipra Singh, Head, Technology First Practice. We have a history of firsts with our clients. It is a track record that underscores our ability to help clients break through–whether it’s launching an independent forum of business leaders to better US-India trade relations, creating the first-ever Alexa Skill for CEO communications, bringing India’s first contactless payments in the ecosystem through Visa or featuring Google Cloud for the first time in the Leaders Quadrant--we take innovation seriously. We’re known for supporting clients through major inflection points. Whether it’s elevating John Chambers as a world class leader, making first-ever headlines for Nutanix in the B2B space, springing Google Cloud from the Challenger to the Leaders quadrant, transforming Visa into a fintech brand, and many others, this is what we do best.

Consumer Connection

The Consumer Connect clientele at Ruder Finn comprises Lifestyle, Entertainment, Luxury, Fashion, Food & Beverage, Consumer Electronics, Automotive, Travel & Hospitality, among many others. We believe that no one size fits all. We focus on developing insight-led creative strategies to empower customers make informed decisions. Our ideas are deeply rooted in culture, our campaigns are social by design and our outreach finds its way into traditional channels organically. We create storytelling formats that are integrated and highly personalised for paid, owned and earned media. Our core competencies include media relations, social media marketing, influencer engagement, celebrity endorsements, content marketing, events and more.

cooperate Reputation

Businesses today operate in a VUCA world, the one witnessing intense scrutiny from media, investors, activists and regulators. At the same time, there are seismic forces surrounding them – be it technology or security, cultural or political shifts, and much more. Ruder Finn India helps position companies for success in this fast-changing world by building reputation and admiration among external stakeholders and the public, as well as deep emotional engagement among employees and partners. Our unique combination of domain knowledge and communication expertise enables us to drive storytelling in a unique way. With a corporate team consisting of former journalists, MBAs, bankers, lawyers, integrated marketing professionals, we’re able to bisect our client’s business, strategy and context for communication and drive the narrative without any misalignment of the messaging across platforms and stakeholders. With our own and network offices across India and our dual Headquarters in China and the US, we’re able to ensure proximity to the influencers that matter the most besides pre-empting risks for our communication programme.

Health and Wellness

As technological innovations disrupt the health and wellness sector, consumer experiences are becoming more personal and revolutionising the way one receives, delivers, measures and views “CARE.” Our multi-skilled teams are trusted advisors who create path breaking multifaceted campaigns across pharma, tech, consumer & public health, eHealth, skincare, nutrition and fitness verticals. Combined with our global experience of over 60 years, we have generated understanding and demand among consumers, patients, healthcare professionals and decision-makers for healthcare products, services and technological advances that lead to longer, healthier lives.

RFI Studios

Through RFI Studios, Ruder Finn provides tailored-made award-winning strategy to our clients. We generate unique stories that speak to a multi-screen generation by exploring immersive media. We create digitally native and content-driven solutions--made simple by design and we believe in the same. From tried and true CEO speeches and newsletters to developing content for digital and social, we’re looking at new ways to create content that connects and builds relationships with your key audiences. We conceptulise dynamic offerings for brands with solutions like Chatbot, Live Feed, Web App, Cinemagraph, 2D/3D Animation & Virtual Reality. From digital and video content to infographics and websites, our work is honed to ensure your words, and your voice, matter. We are multicultural and multinational, with over six nationalities represented in India, Hong Kong, US, UK, SG, & China.

Emerging Technologies

The practice is based out of India and operates globally, using deep tech to analyse data and deliver streamlined campaigns for our clients. Ruder Finn is dedicated to leveraging technology and nothing prepares brands for success better than data-driven analysis and detailed insights. Whether it’s to better understand the effect of a campaign, to deep-dive into the user journey or to polish your strategic decisions, an investment in web analytics & web development using technologies like AI & ML have become essential for today’s modern marketeer. Important business trends, such as the rise of sales and marketing automation, the shift to influencer-driven buying, and the convergence of corporate and brand reputation, require a fully integrated and technology driven go-to-market plan. That's where we come in.
